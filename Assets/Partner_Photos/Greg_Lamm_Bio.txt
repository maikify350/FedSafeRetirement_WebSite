Greg Lamm RFC®, FRC℠
Founding and Senior Partner | FedSafe Retirement

Greg Lamm has made it his life’s work to educate and assist people with important financial decisions since 2003, and has worked exclusively with federal employees since 2008.

After helping thousands of federal employees personally, Greg leveraged his expertise and has co-founded multiple companies over the decades that specialize in delivering much needed assistance to this underserved group of hard-working people. Through his plain English, no nonsense approach, Greg has played a key role in designing the seminars, webinars, and clear guidance that tens of thousands of federal employees have relied on to secure a comfortable and predictable retirement.

When he’s not educating and coaching the workforce that helps our government employees retire without surprises and mistakes, Greg enjoys running marathons and ultramarathons throughout the U.S., playing guitar, and raising his three sons to be strong, independent young men. His discipline, outside-the-box thinking, and attention to detail are what make him the father and business leader that he knows others have come to rely on.

Greg’s professional philosophy is simple. “Understand the needs of the market that you serve, and the needs of the team that you work with, and do everything in your power with honesty and integrity to help them both succeed.”
