    Why Agencies Host These Sessions

    Federal retirement decisions are layered and often misunderstood. When employees approach retirement without clarity, it can lead to:

    • Delayed filings
    • Confusion around benefits elections
    • Repeated HR questions
    • Avoidable mistakes

    A structured briefing provides clear, federal-specific guidance so employees understand:

    • Their options
    • The timing implications
    • The decisions that matter most

    Why You Should Have FedSafe Retirement Host YOUR Sessions

    Each session is tailored to your workforce, but commonly includes:

    • FERS and CSRS pension fundamentals
    • Retirement eligibility & timing strategy
    • The FERS Special Retirement Supplement
    • TSP contribution and withdrawal considerations
    • FEHB and Medicare coordination
    • Survivor benefit elections
    • Common paperwork pitfalls

    The focus is clarity – not product sales.
