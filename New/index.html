    Managing Your Federal Benefits Takes
    Expert-level Insight.

    A federal retirement is a chain of elections, not a single decision. From choosing the right retirement date to filling out and submitting your Online Retirement Application (ORA), each decision affects the others. FedSafe helps employees across all federal agencies and the U.S. Postal Service see the full picture before any choice is locked in.

    This is not generic planning…

    FedSafe Retirement LLC is a federally registered vendor through SAM.gov, positioned to support U.S. government agencies with retirement education and financial literacy services.

    Every FedSafe consultant holds the Certified Federal Retirement Consultant℠ Professional Designation through FFEBA. Our presentations and consultations are developed in alignment with applicable U.S. Office of Personnel Management (OPM) guidelines.

    We ensure federal employees and retirees make confident, well-timed decisions about their federal retirement benefits.

    These are interconnected federal decisions, and getting one wrong can permanently change outcomes.

    Why FedSafe Exists

    Most federal employees don’t realize how many retirement decisions are irreversible until they are already making them. Deadlines pass quietly. Elections lock in permanently. Policy changes introduce uncertainty.

    FedSafe exists to give federal employees clarity early enough for it to matter, so decisions are made intentionally, not reactively.

    Who We Are

    FedSafe is built by professionals who work exclusively with federal benefits.

    No generalists. No side practices.

    Clients work with experienced specialists who understand federal rules, timelines, and execution, because this is all we do.

    Philosophy & Approach

    Federal retirement planning is not about chasing returns. It is about avoiding preventable mistakes, understanding tradeoffs, and making informed decisions at the right time.

    • Clarity beats complexity.
    • Timing matters more than perfection.
    • Education must come before implementation.

    Our goal is not to leave people thinking about it, our goal is to help them see clearly enough to move forward.

    How We Help

    Guidance may include retirement date planning, benefit elections, health insurance & Medicare coordination, TSP strategy, income planning, and ongoing decision support as circumstances change.

    How The FedSafe Process Works

    Our process is designed to remove uncertainty and help clients focus on the decisions that matter most.

    Each step builds on the previous one, with an emphasis on timing, clarity, and understanding before action.

    It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3!

    Introductory Conversation

    A focused discussion about your federal service history, retirement timeline, and the key decisions ahead. This conversation helps identify whether a structured review would be valuable for your situation. Before any recommendations are made, we make sure we fully understand the landscape – we stop, collaborate, and listen.

    Federal Benefits Review

    A structured review of relevant federal benefits, elections, and deadlines that may affect decisions. Guidance is provided to help you understand tradeoffs, timing, and the sequence of important decisions.

    Next Steps, Implementation & Ongoing Checkpoints

    Clear direction on next steps, including support with elections, paperwork, and coordination of retirement income decisions. Periodic reviews as circumstances, timelines, or policy conditions change.

    Built For Clarity

    The process is structured to help clients understand their options before decisions become final. The emphasis is on timing, tradeoffs, and sequencing, so choices are made intentionally rather than reactively.
